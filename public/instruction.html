Mass Labeling Manual

Objective

You will see screenshots of some websites. The objective is to evaluate visual attractiveness of each website screenshot on a scale from 1 to 10.

Evaluation criteria

Even though we ask for a subjective opinion, there should be some certainty and consistency in your evaluations; so in this test, we ask you to think why you give this particular score on a scale of 1-10 to a website when assessing it.

Websites should be assessed by their appearance, not by the information they contain.

We do not evaluate how informative or modern a website is, so its evaluation should not be affected by the factors like these:

  • a website is useful to some particular audience of users
  • a website contains a picture of an awesome Ferrari or your favorite Pokemon
  • a website is adaptive, made using modern frameworks, has a convenient menu

What should influence the evaluation:

  • a website is pleasant to look at
  • well-matched colors are chosen for a website
  • a website contains high-quality images

We hope for your conscientiousness and impartiality but being over critical is not necessary too.

Instruction

Hitting a number on the keyboard gives a score on a scale of 1-10 where 1 is the minimum score, 10 is the maximum score. The 0 number on the keyboard is the score of 10.

To confirm the score and move on the next screen, press Enter.

To return to the previous screenshot, hit the Backspace ← key.

If you see a broken screenshot, press the X button instead of the assessment buttons — this marks the screenshot as broken. Don’t forget to mark screenshots as broken if there’s something wrong with an image.
